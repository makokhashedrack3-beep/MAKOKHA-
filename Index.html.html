Certificate Forgery Crisis in Uganda

Uganda has recently experienced a rise in counterfeit academic certificates affecting employers across Kampala and beyond. Many organisations have unknowingly hired individuals using forged UCE, UACE, and degree documents, leading to financial loss and reputational damage.

Steps to Verify a Certificate

  1. Collect the candidate’s original certificate
  2. Check for visible security features
  3. Visit the UNEB verification portal
  4. Submit certificate details online or physically
  5. Await official confirmation from UNEB

Documents Verified by UNEB

  • UCE Certificates
  • UACE Certificates
  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE)
  • Diploma Transcripts
  • Degree Certificates
  • Vocational Training Certificates

UNEB Public Announcement

The Uganda National Examinations Board has intensified its efforts to combat the increasing circulation of forged academic documents. Employers are urged to verify all academic credentials before hiring.

UNEB has introduced a digital verification system and strengthened regional verification centres to ensure quick and reliable authentication of certificates. Offenders found using forged documents will face legal consequences.
